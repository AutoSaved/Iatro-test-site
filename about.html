About Us

At iatrolex, your health, comfort and safety are our top priorities, providing expert service in a welcoming environment.

our team is made up of professionals across multiple disciplines allowing us to provide our best service at every level.

Legal

Iatrolex is a leading medico-legal consultancy in South Africa, established in 2017 to provide much needed services and expertise in the area of Medical Law and Medical Malpractice. iatroLex MedicoLegal Consulting was established to provide consulting services to plaintiff and defense attorneys on medical malpractice matters, insurance companies and health care organizations and to assist the courts in achieving outcomes that are morally right and fair, in accordance with the law.
